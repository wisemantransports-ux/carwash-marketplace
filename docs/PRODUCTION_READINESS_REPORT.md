## 2. Market Readiness Shortcomings

### Scalability Bottlenecks
- **Manual Payment Verification**: Subscription activation relies on Admin review of MM screenshots. This is a manual labor risk as you scale beyond 50 partners.
- **Service Payments**: No integrated escrow. Payments happen "off-platform" (Cash/MM direct). The platform relies on business integrity to "Mark Paid."

### Functional Gaps
- **Real-time Comms**: No internal chat. Coordination relies on WhatsApp redirects. 
- **Inventory Sync**: If a car is sold at a physical station, the owner must manually update the app. There is no automated POS integration.
